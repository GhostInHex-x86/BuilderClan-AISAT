FAQ

What is BuilderClan?

A tech community where builders, coders, and designers collaborate on projects and ideas.

Who can join?

Anyone with a passion for building — whether you're a beginner or an expert.

How do I join?

Click the Join button below to connect with us on Discord and start collaborating.

What activities does BuilderClan organize?

We host open-source projects, hackathons, coding challenges, workshops, webinars, and community meetups, as well as networking opportunities and mentorship programs.

Are there any rules for the community?

Yes, we have a set of community rules to ensure a respectful and collaborative environment. You can find them on our official website.

Can I promote my projects or services in the community?

Yes, but please ensure your promotions are relevant and non-spammy. Use designated channels for project showcases.

How do I collaborate on community projects?

Keep an eye on announcements for project opportunities. Once a project is announced, you can apply to join the team or contribute through GitHub.

How do I contact the BuilderClan team?

You can reach us at main.builderclan@gmail.com or use the contact form on our website.
